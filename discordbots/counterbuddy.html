How to use the bot

1. Go into the channel you want the bot to operate in(only one channel per server)

2. Type “CounterBuddy getcountingchannel”

3. Start counting!

How to change the channel it operates in

This only works if you already did the above instructions!

1. Go into the new channel you want the bot to operate in

2. “CounterBuddy newcountingchannel”

3. Start counting in that channel now!
